Why refer to IHL

i.Comprehensive intake & clinician matching

All referrals triaged by a trained team. A structured intake framework matches clients to the right clinician by presentation, complexity, and autonomy.

ii.Depth of clinical capability

15+ AHPRA-registered psychologists across the full spectrum of presentations, including complex, high-risk and comorbid cases.

iii.Timely access & continuity

Referrals reviewed during business hours. Progress reports and clinical correspondence to the treating team, with client consent.

iv.Evidence-based & integrative

CBT, DBT, Schema, EMDR, ACT and other modality-informed approaches. Treatment tailored to presentation and goals.

Referral pathway
01

Submit referral

Via HealthLink, online form, intake email or fax. Direct referrals accepted from psychiatrists and paediatricians.

02

Clinician matching

Detailed clinical review by presentation, complexity, age, comorbidities, and clinician expertise. Thorough by design.

03

Appointment & feedback

Appointment options confirmed after intake review. Progress reports at clinically appropriate intervals, with consent.
