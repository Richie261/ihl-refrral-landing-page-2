    What’s required for a valid Medicare referral

    A valid Medicare referral includes patient name, date of birth and address; symptoms or diagnosis; current medications; the number of treatment services; and a statement on whether the patient has an MHTP or PAMP. Signed and dated by the referring practitioner.
